Welcome to Boomerang Giving

The generation known for standing up is giving back

As Baby Boomers,we've lived through some of the most memorable events in our country's history. Like it or not, we were shaped one way or another by the tides of social change. And so was this country. It's really no surprise that the same generation that has been known for activism is the largest group of contributors to non-profits than any other generation today1.
Boomerang Giving is a movement organized by Boomers to give our senior discounts to communities and people in need. Each year,3.5 million people tum 65. At this stage in our lives, it just feels night to be the generation that takes the lead and starts this movement of us-- and all retirees-- giving back our senior discounts to people in need.
Help us ignite this movement today and make your pledge to boomerang your discounts.
